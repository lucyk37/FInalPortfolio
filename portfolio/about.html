About Me

Hi, I'm Lucy. Growing up, I thought I'd become a singer or an actress. Who would've guess I'd get into coding? Certaintly not me. But, I'd like to think I'm doing a pretty good job for someone who just started not that long ago. Being someone who had no experience in coding, it was very challenging for me and honestly it still is. My "official" work experience consisted of waitressing, being a barista, and working in multiple grocery stores. Yeah... the two aren't similar at all unless you count the interacting with customers part.

With that being said, I offer two main services. Website Design and Graphic Design. I am familiar with HTML, CSS, and a little Javascript. I also am familiar with Photoshop and Illustrator. Check out the 'works' page to see my designs. If you like any of them, be sure to send me a request in the 'services' page.

As this page updates over time, I hope you'll be able to see my progress in my journey to becoming a Web Developer. See you soon!
